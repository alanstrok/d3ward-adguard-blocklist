! Description: Blocks common ad script filenames everywhere with high priority
! Updated: 2025-09-19
! Syntax: AdGuard/uBO

/\/pagead(?:\/js\/.*|\.js)(?:\?.*)?$/\$script,important
/\/gpt\.js(?:\?.*)?$/\$script,important
/\/(?:adserver|adtag|ads|advert|advertising)(?:\.min)?\.js(?:\?.*)?$/\$script,important
/\/(?:promo|sponsor)s?\.js(?:\?.*)?$/\$script,important

! ---- Generic directory patterns (regex) ----
/\/(?:ads?|adserver|advertising)\/.*\.js(?:\?.*)?$/\$script,important
